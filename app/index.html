Understanding the Clinical and Economic Data of Botulinum Toxin in Chronic Migraines

Examine evidence-based strategies to help patients with migraine, and demonstrate onabotulinumtoxin. A injection's potential benefits and appropriate use in patients with chronic migraine. Identify collaborative management plans with prescribers who wish to use onabotulinumtoxinA, emphasizing patient education.

Expires: Sep 6,2018 | Credits: 1.00 | UAN: 0290-000-17-133-H01-P

The Expanding Health Benefits of Prebiotics and Probiotics

LAs our understanding of the complex relationship between the gut microbiota and human health evolves, the number of potential uses for probiotics grows. In order to help patients decide if a probiotic will be beneficial, pharmacists need to know the evidence supporting the use of probiotics for a plethora of indications. They must also provide advice on choosing products, including how to avoid quality issues such as contamination, sub-potency, and stability. This activity will provide pharmacists with an approach to evaluating these issues.

Expires: Sep 6,2018 | Credits: 1.00 | UAN: 0290-000-17-133-H01-P
